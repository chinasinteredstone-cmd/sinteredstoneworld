Returns & Terms of Service

At Foshan Wharton Building Material Co., Ltd., we maintain the highest quality standards for our Sintered Stone and MCM Soft Ceramic series. As a global supplier, our return policy aligns with international stone industry standards (referencing protocols used by leaders like Cosentino and Neolith).

1. Acceptance & Inspection upon Delivery

Clients are required to inspect all products immediately upon arrival. Apparent defects or quantity discrepancies must be noted on the delivery receipt and reported to chinasinteredstone@gmail.com within 48 hours. Once products are moved to a job site or installation has commenced, the products are deemed accepted in their current condition.

2. Non-Returnable Items (Custom Fabrication)

Consistent with industry practices, the following items are strictly non-returnable:

  • Products that have been cut-to-size, fabricated, polished, or altered in any way.
  • Special project orders or custom-manufactured MCM Soft Ceramic batches.
  • Installed products (Installation constitutes final acceptance).

3. Return of Standard Stock Items

Returns for un-fabricated, standard stock items in original export packaging are accepted within 14 days of delivery, subject to:

  • An automatic 25% Restocking Fee applied to the product value.
  • Client bearing all return freight, insurance, and export/import duties.

4. Tonal & Batch Variations

Please note that Sintered Stone and Soft Ceramic materials may exhibit natural variations in color, tone, and texture between different production batches. Such variations are inherent to the manufacturing process and are not considered manufacturing defects.

5. Damaged Shipments

For items damaged during ocean or air freight, please submit photographic evidence of both the crate/packaging and the damaged slabs. We will facilitate a replacement or credit following verification with the logistics carrier.
