Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

At Foshan Wharton Building Material Co., Ltd., we strive to ensure total satisfaction for our global stone cladding clients. This policy applies to all products purchased via https://www.whartonbuilding.com.

1. Custom Orders (Sintered Stone/Slabs)

Due to the customized nature of our sintered stone slabs and custom-cut products, we cannot accept returns or provide refunds for these items once production has started or they have been shipped, unless there is a verifiable manufacturing defect.

2. Shipping Damage

Stone products are fragile. If your order arrives damaged during transit, please contact us at chinasinteredstone@gmail.com within 48 hours of receipt. You must provide clear photos of the damaged items and the packaging. We will provide a free replacement or appropriate compensation after verification.

3. Standard Stock Items

For non-customized standard stock products, returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery. Items must be in their original packaging and condition. The customer is responsible for all return shipping costs and insurance.
